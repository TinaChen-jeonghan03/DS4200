Seventeen’s unique team structure is often described as a “self-producing idol group,” because many of their songs are composed and written by the members themselves. Woozi, for example, plays a significant role in songwriting and production, while Hoshi often contributes heavily to choreography. The group’s synergy is further enhanced by their three specialized sub-units:

  • Hip-Hop Unit: S.Coups (leader), Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon
  • Vocal Unit: Jeonghan, Joshua, Woozi, DK, and Seungkwan
  • Performance Unit: Hoshi, Jun, The8, and Dino

Each sub-unit focuses on their respective areas—rap, vocals, and dance—allowing the group to excel across the board. Beyond musical artistry, Seventeen also actively engages in variety shows, concerts, and fan-oriented content that highlight their playful chemistry and warm bond. They regularly release behind-the-scenes videos, vlogs, and special projects to keep Carats connected and updated.

One notable achievement in Seventeen’s career has been their ability to maintain a consistent presence on global music charts—testament to their international popularity. Their tours often sell out stadiums, bringing fans together from all over the world. For newcomers, diving into Seventeen’s music can be an exciting journey filled with energetic performances, heartfelt ballads, and an ever-growing discography. If you haven’t checked out their latest releases or watched their live stages, you’re definitely missing out on one of K-pop’s most dynamic groups!
